## Sharing as We Go

The DiffUSE Project is built on Astera Institute's commitment to 
radical transparency. We share our data, methods, and reasoning 
continuously -- not just after results are final. In line with 
Astera's [Open Science Policy](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15548989), 
all of our research outputs are designed to be **FAIR**: Findable, 
Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable.

We believe that unlocking protein dynamics requires not just new 
methods, but new ways of working together openly. By publishing 
our work as it happens, we maximize its potential for broad reach, 
impact, and reuse -- and we invite others to build on what we find.
